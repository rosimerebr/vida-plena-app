Daily Challenge
Sunlight
Spend some time outdoors to soak in the natural sunlight and boost your mood.
Water
Keep yourself hydrated throughout the day by drinking plenty of water.
Air
Get fresh air by spending time outside or by ventilating your living spaces.
Healthy Food
Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and legumes.
Physical Exercise
Engage in regular physical activity that suits your abilities and preferences.
Temperance
Practice moderation in all aspects of life, avoiding harmful substances and behaviors.
Rest
Ensure you get adequate sleep each night and take time to relax and unwind during the day.
