About Vida Plena

Vida Plena is a holistic wellness app designed to help users build healthy habits and achieve a balanced lifestyle through daily challenges, habit tracking, and motivational content.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Empower individuals to live a fuller, healthier life by promoting positive habits and spiritual growth.
Vision: To be the leading platform for holistic well-being, integrating physical, mental, and spiritual health.

Main Features
  • Daily habit tracking and progress visualization
  • Spiritual messages and Bible verses
  • Comprehensive reports and statistics
  • User-friendly interface and offline support
